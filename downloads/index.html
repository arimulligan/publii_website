Your copy of our videos

Downloads page - On this downloads page you can view and download our videos. To download an entire series on your computer, click on the folder and then “download” in the top right-hand corner. On your phone, click next to the series name. To download a single episode, click on the series name to enter its folder, then click on the episode and then on the download icon in the bottom right-hand corner of the screen, and finally on “save to device.”

If you're downloading an entire folder, you'll need space on your device and an application capable of opening the zip file that arrives at your home. Once you have the files on your device, you can give them to others without needing an internet connection by using your device's Bluetooth sharing function.
